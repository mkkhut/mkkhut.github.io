We strongly believe in reviving forgotten pieces of everyday life and restoring them back to life, with style and function. Experimenting with such furniture to extend ourselves and create quirky pieces making a statement in any space. Reusing, repurposing and up-cycling remain as key elements of our design world.


Some simple ideas to repurpose furniture:

    A waste tin can be converted into a seating by retaining the structures as shown in image.

    Waste pipes can be reused for planters, shoe unit, ceiling rustic designs.

    Reuse of old tyers for furniture.

    Old plastic bottles can be used as a decorative hanging lamp.
