Architectural Design Co.

Malvika Khut is a Mumbai based Interior Designer. After receiving her degree, she spent the following years creating visual displays for various clients. She used her knowledge to home design and eventually created her own home staging company, Malvika Khut Design Company LLC.

“The first love of my life was style. For me, it is an all-consuming affection for how you live your life. I am a visual addict and I aspire to surround myself with everything beautiful, showing others that it is about conveying that 'something special’ that transforms an object into an experience. I often see the beauty in places overlooked. I integrate those pieces into a collection that represents my authentic point of view. In doing so, I hope that others will not only appreciate but understand that this is my gift to share with the world.”

“We all have an addiction, a go-to and an escape: this is mine. This is me. This is my everyday everything.”.

Why Choose Me

"When you build a personal space with someone, you inevitably build a relationship with them and that is what I value most!"
If you’re here, it’s because you’ve “heard of me” or googled me, or just flat out stumbled upon my site. But, if you’re choosing me as your interior designer…

Choose me because you love my style, the photos of other work I’ve done, and you resonate with who I am.

This is what’s most important.

When choosing someone to decorate your home, make sure you thoroughly review their portfolio.

  • Do you love their style?
  • Do you see just ONE photo you like, or do you love them all?
  • Do you like their vibe? If not, chances are, they aren’t for you.

Every designer has a style. It should be one of the biggest reasons why you choose one designer over another.

My style can be described as chic and chill. I want your home to look like a page straight out of a magazine, except one where you aren’t afraid to let your hair down, put your feet up, and if you happen to spill a glass of wine (or two), its not the end of the world. While I understand this isn’t a look for everyone, it’s the look my clients love. If you want me to copy the style of another designer (and for cheaper), I’m probably not the designer for you. Go to the source. Find a designer whose style you like. This might not be me, and that’s OKAY!! I promise. There’s so much talent out there. Find someone whose style fits yours!
